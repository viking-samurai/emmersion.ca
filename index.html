Coding Lessons

Emmersion.ca is a tutoring company that specializes in teaching literacy and technology to school-aged students. At Emmersion, I offer courses in JavaScript programming and website development, specifically designed for school-aged students and their families. Coding and programming is now one of the most sought after skillsets in North America, especially as automation moves into the workforce. Why not start learning today?

Collaborative Learning

Collaborative learning is a new look at group learning that promotes both teamwork and individual growth, for students looking to learn a similar goal at a similar pace. At Emmersion, all of my courses are offered as either one on one instruction, or as collaborative learning opportunities, with up to four students per class. I also come to you with my own simple setup, wherever that may be, to make it easy for you stay working.

The Growth Mindset

Beginner Coding

Beginner Coding introduces students to coding and programming. It starts with HTML5 and CSS3, and prepares students for learning JavaScript at the advanced level.

Advanced Coding

Advanced Coding picks up from the beginner level, and moves students through more complex concepts, while introducing JavaScript, a popular programming language.

Creative Collaborations

Essay Engineer

Essay Engineer is a writing course for developing voice and thoroughness of communication. The course looks at writing for a purpose to enhance student ability.
