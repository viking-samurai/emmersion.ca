Essay Engineer

Learn to write a properly formatted essay.

Coding and programming are one of the most sought after skillsets in today's job market, and for good reason. They are a highly technical skillset that requires practitioners to think both mathematically and linguistically, generally at the same time. As AI and automation continue to move into the workforce, those who have the ability to work with modern technology are able to make businesses more efficient, and then become invaluable to that business in the process. Learning coding and programming earlier in life helps ensure that you or your children won't get left behind.

Why learn through web development?

The Internet offers a unique way for students to learn coding and programming through modern web development. The trade promotes cognitive growth, is free to set-up and start learning, and provides learners with a unique medium for art and self-expression. With E-mmersion.ca, Beginner coding starts students off with the basics: HTML5 and CSS3. Students learn to control form before function, and move slowly through the different technologies that go into web development. This helps learners get a feel for how the various langauges are all connected, and how they work together to create a whole.

Withe E-mmersion.ca's advanced coding classes, students pick up where our beginner courses leave off, continuing with HTML5 and CSS3, but adding JavaScript programming to the mix as well. JavaScript is currently both the most common and the most sought after programming language in modern software development, and, given that it is considered ‘the language of the Internet’, it is likely to remain a dominant programming language for years to come. The courses below are designed to be taken as collaborative learning opportunities in either English or French, but can also be prepared for one on one tutoring as well.
